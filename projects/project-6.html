Portfolio Diversification via Geopolitical Arbitrage

Business Viability and Pricing Strategy

The strategic challenge

The Russia Ukraine conflict pushed energy costs sharply higher in some regions and lower in others. A ceramics business needed to decide fast whether to hold its position or shift into a new product line.

What the project delivers

Using Porter's Five Forces and Ansoff's Growth Matrix, the analysis identified a window to pivot part of the ceramics portfolio into glassware, taking advantage of a German energy cost arbitrage created by the conflict.

This turned a period of market uncertainty into a source of new margin, by moving quickly on an opportunity that most competitors were too slow to see.
