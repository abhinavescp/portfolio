E-Commerce Webpage Optimization

Sayano Deutschland GmbH, Digital Sales Strategy

The strategic challenge

Direct to consumer product listings on platforms like Media Markt and Euronics were not converting as well as they should. The content needed to be tested and improved with real evidence, not guesswork.

What the project delivers

The project runs structured A/B tests on A plus content and keyword choices, measuring which version of a listing actually sells better. A Voice of Customer feedback loop was also built, so real customer comments fed directly back into future content changes.

The result is a repeatable process for improving product listings, driven by data rather than opinion, with a clear return on investment for every content change made.
